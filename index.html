The Problem

Healthcare is broken by missing information

Every day, millions of patients are treated without their doctors knowing their full history. This causes delays, errors, and in emergencies — it costs lives.

😰
4 min

Patients Can't Remember

Doctors spend 4+ minutes every consultation collecting history the patient can't recall — medications, past surgeries, allergies, and dosages. Critical information gets missed.

📋
67%

Records Are Fragmented

67% of patients have records scattered across multiple hospitals, clinics, and labs. No single place holds the full picture. Doctors make decisions with incomplete data.

🚨
1 in 5

Emergencies Are Dangerous

1 in 5 emergency patients cannot communicate their allergies or current medications. Wrong treatment based on unknown allergies kills thousands of patients every year.

How It Works

From upload to diagnosis in seconds

Four steps. Any record type. Any doctor. Anywhere in the world.

📸

Patient Uploads

Patient photographs any medical document — prescription, lab report, discharge summary, X-ray. Any format accepted.

🤖

AI Reads Everything

Chitti AI reads the document, extracts diagnosis, medications, allergies, lab values, and dates. Patient confirms. Profile updated instantly.

📷

Doctor Scans QR

Patient shows their AHP QR code. Doctor scans it. Patient grants consent on their phone. Access is live in seconds.

✅

Doctor Sees Everything

Full structured health summary. Allergies first. AI-generated clinical paragraph. All records, vitals, history — ready in seconds.

Security & Privacy

You own your data. Full stop.

AHP is built on the principle that health data belongs to the patient — not the hospital, not the doctor, and not us. Every architectural decision enforces this.

🔐

Encrypted Always

AES-256 encryption at rest. TLS 1.3 in transit. Encryption keys stored in your device's secure enclave — not our servers.

✅

Consent Required

No doctor sees your data without you actively granting access. Every access requires you to confirm on your phone in real time.

📋

Every Access Logged

You see a permanent log of every person who viewed your data — doctor name, clinic, what they saw, date and time. Nothing is hidden.

🚫

Revoke Instantly

One tap revokes any doctor's access. It is blocked at the database level immediately — they cannot view your records again from that second.

🌐

DPDP Compliant

Built for India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. All consent operations logged with timestamp, purpose, and duration.

🆔

Aadhaar Safe

Your Aadhaar number is never stored — anywhere. Only the verification result is kept: name, DOB, verified. The number is never touched by our system.

📵

No Ads. Ever.

Your health data is never used for advertising, never sold, and never shared with any third party for any commercial purpose.

🚨

Offline Emergency

Life-critical data cached locally. Emergency mode shows your allergies and medications with zero internet connection, always.
